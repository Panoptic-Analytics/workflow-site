DocuSign

Paperless, fast and compliant with regulations

Using DocuSign, you can share a document with your clients for them to sign electronically - and then they simply send it straight back. Accelerate the execution process, lower your operating costs and provide a superior client experience, while reducing transitional risk and increasing the transparency of your workflow.

Each document is stored against their record in intelliflo office, and a digital audit trail makes regulatory processes much easier.
